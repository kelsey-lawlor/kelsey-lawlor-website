About Me

I’m a digital strategist and web product specialist with over 7 years of experience supporting healthcare and public health organizations. My work focuses on translating complex health data and regulatory requirements into accurate, accessible, and user‑friendly digital products. I enjoy bringing together cross‑functional teams to deliver polished solutions that improve access to information and empower people to make informed decisions.

Throughout my career I’ve partnered closely with clinicians, policy leaders, developers and communicators to build data‑driven products, lead quality assurance processes, and optimize content for search. I’m dedicated to creating digital experiences that are compliant with accessibility standards, grounded in human‑centered design, and backed by robust analytics.
